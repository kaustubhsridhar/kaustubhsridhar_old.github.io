1. Are you concerned about Batch Normalization? The effects of BN on Tranfer, Adversarial and Meta-learning. (ESE 546 : Principles of Deep Learning, Fall 2020)

Prof. Pratik Chaudhary

In this project, we study the effects of batch normalization's running statistics and affine parameters on transfer learning (from Imagenet to GTSRB), adversarial datasets and learning (with a custom dataset of stop sign images and their black box attacked versions created in the PRECISE group and on Carlini Wagner whitebox attacked images). To do so, we create 5 custom experiments exploring various BN layer situations with disparate batch size usage in training, differentiated focus on different layers and usage of different datasets. We also analyze the effect of BN running statistics usage in few-shot learning on the Omniglot dataset with MAML.
